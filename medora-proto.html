HOW IT WORKS

One upload, five automated stages.

Every scan moves through the same clinically-ordered pipeline — detection first, explanation second, so nothing is asserted without evidence.

01

Upload

A chest X-ray is submitted through the browser dashboard — no PACS integration required to start.

02

Detect

EfficientNet-B4, fine-tuned on NIH ChestX-ray14, flags multiple co-existing pathologies at once.

03

Explain

Grad-CAM heatmaps and bounding boxes show exactly which pixels drove each finding.

04

Triage

Confidence scores map to Normal, Mild, Moderate, or Severe — urgent cases surface first.

05

Report

A structured, editable draft is generated with findings, impression, and a mandatory AI disclaimer.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT

Built past the point where most diagnostic AI stops.

Most systems hand back a label. VaidyaAI stays in the loop through explanation, triage, and reporting — with the clinician always the final signature.

◎

No black box

Every prediction ships with a Grad-CAM heatmap and bounding box, so a finding can be checked, not just trusted.

⬡

One unified workflow

Detection, localisation, severity, and reporting live in a single platform — no switching between tools.

✦

Multi-pathology aware

Reports primary and secondary findings together, reflecting how scans actually present in practice.

▲

Severity-first triage

Cases are auto-ranked so the most urgent patient is never waiting behind a routine one.

⬢

Deployable anywhere

A lightweight Docker build runs without specialised hardware — built for district hospitals, not just flagship ones.

✎

Editable, always

The draft report is a starting point. Every diagnosis stays under clinician review before it's signed.
